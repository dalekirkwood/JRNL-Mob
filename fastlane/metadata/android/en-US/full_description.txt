<p>JRNL-Mob is an Android companion for &lt;a href="https://jrnl.sh"&gt;jrnl&lt;/a&gt; — the open-source command-line journaling tool. It gives you mobile access to your existing jrnl journal files through your own WebDAV server.</p>

<p><b>This is a companion app — you need jrnl and a WebDAV server to use it.</b> If you don't already journal with jrnl on your computer, set it up first at &lt;a href="https://jrnl.sh"&gt;jrnl.sh&lt;/a&gt;.</p>

<p><b>How it works</b></p>
<p>jrnl stores your journal as plain text files. JRNL-Mob reads and writes those same files, syncing through your WebDAV server (Nextcloud, ownCloud, etc.). Write on your phone during the day, edit on your laptop with jrnl at night — same file, same format.</p>

<p><b>Features</b></p>
<ul>
<li>🎭 Mood tracking with emoji</li>
<li>📍 Location tagging via GPS</li>
<li>🌤️ Automatic weather (Open-Meteo, no API key)</li>
<li>#️⃣ @tag syntax — jrnl-compatible</li>
<li>🔍 Full-text search</li>
<li>🔄 WebDAV sync (Nextcloud, ownCloud, any WebDAV)</li>
<li>📄 Plain text format — never locked in</li>
<li>🎨 Material 3 design with dark mode</li>
<li>🚫 100% free software — GPL-3.0, zero proprietary deps</li>
</ul>

<p>Your journal, your server, your rules.</p>
